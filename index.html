about {

Johannes is an independent sound artist based in Eichstätt, Germany. While studying Audio Engineering, he developed a strong passion for sound design and his productions turned into more and more unique soundscapes. He is driven by the ambition to discover new, profound sounds that move and captivate the listener. His work is also strongly inspired by his fascination with dreams. He believes dreams create feelings and moods that are often hard to find in real life, and he tries to bring these unique emotions into his sound projects.

His style is atmospheric, experimental, minimalistic, and provocative, as he challenges traditional ideas in music and sound. Since Johannes avoids following set rules, his creativity is without limits, and he feels more connected to his artistic identity.

Believing that money should not dominate art, Johannes prefers the concept of “pay what you want”. That's why he leaves it up to the supporters of his projects on Bandcamp to decide for themselves how much his art is worth to them.

}
