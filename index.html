about

Johannes is an independent sound artist from Munich, and his work is strongly inspired by his fascination with dreams. He believes dreams create feelings and moods that are often hard to find in real life. He tries to bring these unique emotions into his sound projects.

His style is atmospheric, experimental, minimalistic, and provocative, as he challenges traditional ideas in music and sound. Johannes avoids following set rules, believing that doing so limits creativity and takes away from the artist’s individuality.

Imagine being an artist but having to work according to someone else’s expectations. You might feel good about meeting their demands, but are you truly happy with the result? In his eyes, there is a trend toward making music and sound more alike, which shapes what people expect to hear. This might lead many artists to limit their own creativity to fit these norms.

Artists who follow these rules may get approval from the industry and society, but they risk losing their artistic identity. Those who break free from these standards are more connected to their true selves and contribute to a wider range of sounds. Questioning their own work and its authenticity helps expand the way we experience sound, bringing more variety and richness to art.
