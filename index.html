about

Johannes is an independent sound artist based in Munich. His fascination with dreams greatly influences his work. For him, dreams can create atmospheres that are often unattainable in real life. Johannes aims to evoke these unique feelings in his projects by trying to translate them into sound. He expresses his ideas in an experimental, minimalistic, and provocative way, questioning and intentionally disregarding conventional ideals in music and sound that distort and restrict creativity.
